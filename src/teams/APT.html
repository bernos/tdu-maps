ASTANA PRO TEAM

THE RIDERS: DID YOU KNOW?

Muravyev, Bozic and Guarnieri all join Astana for the 2012 season, making their team debut at the 2012 Santos Tour Down Under.

Guarnieri was selected to add to the team's sprinting stocks, perhaps making him one to watch vying for points for the Jayco Sprint Jersey.

Assan Bazayev is the 2008 Kazakhstan Road Race Champion and finished eighth in 2010 in the UCI World Road Championships.

Jacop Guarnieri has won two stages of the Tour of Poland during his professional cycling career.

Francesco Masciarelli won the Tour of Japan in 2007 and took stage honours on Stage 5 of the Tour Mediterranean.

In 2011, Valentin Iglinskiy won stage two of the Tour of Turkey.

THE TEAM: DID YOU KNOW?

At the end of the 2010 cycling season, Astana was the third highest ranked cycling team in the world.

Astana attained UCI ProTeam status in its inaugural year, 2007.

Since the team's inception in 2007 it has been based in Switzerland (2007), Luxembourg (2008) and Kazakhstan (2009 - present).
