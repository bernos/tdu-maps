KATUSHA TEAM

THE RIDERS: DID YOU KNOW?

Maxim Belkov will join Team Katusha in 2012 from Vacansoleil DCM.

Belkov rode the 2011 Santos Tour Down Under.

Giampaolo Caruso is a Santos Tour Down Under stage winner, taking honours on Stage 5 in 2003 when riding for ONCE-Eroski.

Xavier Florencio finished seventh overall in the 2003 Tour Down Under.

Oscar Freire is a three time World Road Race Champion (1999, 2001 and 2004).

He is a winner of four stages at the Tour de France, a winner of the Tour de France's Green Jersey (2008), a winner of seven stages of Vuelta a Espana and three times winner of the 'Classicissima' Milan - San Remo (2004, 2007 and 2010).

In the 2011 season Oscar Freire won two stages of Vuelta a Andalucia and six more podium places (at stages of World Tour races Vuelta Ciclista al Pais Vasco, Tour de Romandie, Tour de Suisse and Vuelta a Espana).

Luca Paolini is a Vuelta a Espana stage winner, taking honours on stage 12 in the 2006 event.

Gatis Smukulis joins Team Katusha from HTC-Highroad in 2012. He is the 2011 Latvian National Time Trial Champion.

Eduard Vorganov finished 7th overall in the General Classification at the 2010 Santos Tour Down Under.

THE TEAM: DID YOU KNOW?

Team Katusha made their UCI ProTour debut in 2009 at the 2009 Santos Tour Down Under.

The team is organised by the Russian Global Cycling Project foundation.

During its third season the Katusha team reached 67 podium places (with 22 victories), winning World Tour races in three continents: Europe, Asia and Oceania.

At the 2010 Santos Tour Down Under, Katusha finished fourth in the Brilliant Blend Winning Team classification.
