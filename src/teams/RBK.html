THE RIDERS: DID YOU KNOW?

Luis Leon Sanchez won the overall Santos Tour Down Under in 2005.

Sanchez is a three time Tour de France stage winner (2008, 2009, 2011) and a three time Spanish National Time Trial Champion (2008, 2010, 2011).

Michael 'Bling' Matthews made his professional cycling debut with Rabobank in 2011.

Matthews is a Santos Tour Down Under stage winner (2011 race, Stage 3) and finished fourth overall.

Mark Renshaw joins the Rabobank team in 2012 and is considered one of the best lead out men in the world, spending the last few years at HTC-Highroad with Mark Cavendish.

Mark Renshaw is a Tour Down Under stage winner (2008 event, Stage 1) and the winner of the Down Under Classic in 2007.

2011 was a brilliant year for Renshaw with him wining the overall Tour of Qatar, a stage win on Stage 1 TTT Giro d'Italia and Stage 5 of the Tour of Britain.

Graeme Brown is a four time Santos Tour Down Under stage winner (2001, 2003, 2008 and 2009).

Graeme Brown was third on Stage 2 of the 2011 Santos Tour Down Under.

Brown is a dual Olympic and Commonwealth Games gold medallist. As a sprint specialist he holds the sprint jersey title at the Tour of Langkawi in 2003 and 2005 and the Sprint Jersey overall classification in 2001 at the Santos Tour Down Under

Tom Leezer held the lead in the mountains at the 2009 Tour of Spain, Stages 2 and 3.

In 2007 Thomas Leezer took honours to win the U23 Dutch Road Race making him the U23 National Champion for that year.

Jos Van Emden is 2010 Dutch National Time Trial Champion taking honours in 2010.

In 2011 Jos came fifth overall in the Eneco Tour.

Wilco Kelderman won the 2011 Tour of Norway and is the 2011 Dutch national Time Trial U23 Champion.

THE TEAM: DID YOU KNOW?

Rabobank is Holland's largest financial services provider with more than 9 million accounts.

Rabobank invests in cycling. This investment is not limited to sponsoring professional teams, but also extends to promoting and supporting recreational cycling in its broadest sense throughout the Netherlands. All these activities are carried out under the auspices of the 'Rabo Cycling Plan'.

The Rabo Cycling Plan was established in 1996 as a key spearhead of the strategy for attaining an improved positioning for Rabobank.
