THE TEAM: DID YOU KNOW?

The Liquigas team was founded in 1999.

The Liquigas team had an amazing 2010 season, placing riders on the final podium of two of the year's three grand tours (Giro d'Italia and Vuelta a Espana)

Liquigas is committed to the development of the future champions of cycling and runs Camp
