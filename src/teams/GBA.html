THE RIDERS: DID YOU KNOW?

Ryder Hesjedal finished 11th overall at the 2009 Santos Tour Down Under.

Ryder Hesjedal finished seventh overall at the 2010 Tour de France, with this result making him the highest finishing Canadian cyclist in 22 years.

Hesjedal is a Vuelta a Espana stage winner (Stage 12 in 2009) and Canadian National Time Trial Champion (2007).

Jack Bauer is the 2010 New Zealand National Road Race Champion.

Bauer won Stage 2 of the Tour of Utah in 2011.

Robbie Hunter became the first South African to compete in the Tour de France.

Robbie Hunter has won stages at the 1999 and 2001 Vuelta a Espana and the 2007 Tour de France, the overall title at the 2004 Tour of Qatar and the sprint claissifcation at the 2004 Tour de Suisse.

Nathan Hass will join Garmin-Barracuda in 2012 making his WorldTour debut at the Santos Tour Down Under.

In 2011, Haas won the Herald Sun Tour and the Japan Cup.

Heinrich Haussler is a Tour de France, Vuelta a Espana and Paris-Nice stage winner

In 2007, Andreas Klier won Stage 1 of the Vuelta a Espana.

Martijn Maaskant finished fourth overall in the Paris-Roubaix in 2008.

THE TEAM: DID YOU KNOW?

Team Garmin-Transitions and Barracuda joined forces for the 2012 season to create a new team that has been known as Garmin-Barracuda. The team sponsors share the key philosophies of developing the next generation of cycling champions and declare an unwavering commitment to ethical sport.

The goals for 2012 continue to derive from the ethos of the organization, that of team achievement over that of the individual. This philosophy saw the team reach new heights in 2011, from the Classics to the Grand Tours and beyond.

The team had their strongest season in the history of the team in 2011, and will be looking to build on that success in 2012.
