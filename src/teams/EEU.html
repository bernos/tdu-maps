EUSKALTEL - EUSKADI

THE RIDERS: DID YOU KNOW?

In 2009 Romain Sicard won the U23 UCI World Championship Road Race. In 2010, he joined Euskaltel Euskadi as the only second non-Spanish rider in the team's history.

Sicard finished 51st overall in the 2010 Santos Tour Down Under.

Gorka Izagirre finished 12th overall at the 2011 Santos Tour Down Under and seventh on Stage 3 of this year's race, making him a rider to watch with a bright future.

Gorka Izagirre turned professional in 2010 and in the same year claimed his first professional victory taking honours on the final stage of the Tour of Luxembourg.

Ruben Perez is a Grand Tour riding who will bring a wealth of experience to the 'orange' team, debuting at the Vuelta a Espana in 2006 and the Tour de France in 2007.

He Perez also took honours on stage one of the 2009 Tour of Bavaria.

Pablo Urtasun joined Euskaltel-Euskadi in 2009.

The Euskaltel Euskadi team currently preparing for the Santos Tour Down Under includes three new arrivals for their 2012 season, all debuting with the team in Adelaide. Ricardo García, Victor Cabedo and Adrián Sáez de Arregi are all joining Euskaltel Euskadi from the Orbea Continental team.

THE TEAM: DID YOU KNOW?

Euskaltel Euskadi is commercially sponsored, but also works as an unofficial Basque national team and is partly funded by the Basque Government.

Euskaltel Euskadi is famous for its all-orange team kits.

The team was created in 1994 and although the team is commercially sponsored, it also works as an unofficial Basque national team and is partly funded by the Basque Government.
