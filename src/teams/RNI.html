RADIOSHACK - NISSAN

THE RIDERS: DID YOU KNOW?

Italian sprinter, Daniele Bennati will lead the RadioShack-Nissan team at the 2012 Santos Tour Down Under. This will be his first appearance at the race.

Since 2007, Daniele Bennati has won 10 Grand Tour stages, two at the Tour de France, three at the Giro d'Italia and five at the Vuelta a Espana.

Gerdemann, the 2011 Tour de Luxembourg winner took his biggest victory to date during a mountain stage at the 2007 Tour de France. His strong performance on that day saw him win the yellow jersey for a day.

Jan Bakelants won the 2008 Tour de l'Avenir and rode the Giro d'Italia in 2010, finishing 36th overall.

Jens Voight will be one of two 40 year olds riding in the peloton at the 2012 Santos Tour Down Under.

Voight is a three time Tour de France stage winner and one time Giro d'Italia stage winner.

Tiago Machado finished second overall in the 2011 Giro del Trentino.

New Zealand rider Hayden Roulston is the current New Zealand National Road Race Champion as well as a Commonwealth and Olympic medallist).

Jesse Sergent is an Olympic medallist (bronze) as part of the New Zealand team pursuit squad at the 2008 Beijing Olympics.

Jesse Sergent joined the RadioShack team in 2011.

THE TEAM: DID YOU KNOW?

Team Leopard Trek and RadioShack have joined forces for the 2012 and 2013 cycling seasons. Leopard will continue to hold its existing UCI WorldTour License and the team will be re-branded as RadioShack-Nissan Professional Cycling Team as of 2012.

The RadioShack-Nissan team roster for 2012 is made up of 30 riders, 14 different nationalities.

Major sponsor, RadioShack has 35,000 employees and 6,000 stores across the USA.
