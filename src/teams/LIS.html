THE RIDERS: DID YOU KNOW?

Alessandro Petacchi will be riding the Santos Tour Down Under for the first time in 2012

Alessandro Petacchi is a specialist sprinter who has achieved 48 Grand Tour stage wins to date (6 Tour de France, 22 Giro d'Italia and 20 Vuelta a Espana).

Petacchi is one of only three cyclists to have won stages in all three of the Grand Tours in one year (2003).

Since becoming professional 1996, Petacchi has had 180 victories and appeared at the Giro d'Italia 11 times, Vuleta a Espana 9 times and the Tour de France four times.

Australian, Matthew Lloyd is a new addition to the team roster in 2012.

Matthew Lloyd is the first Australian rider to win the GC for a King of the Mountains classification in a Grand Tour, taking the KOM title at the 2010 Giro d'Italia.

In 2007, Lloyd finished fourth overall at the Santos Tour Down Under and in 2008 was the Australian National Road Race Champion.

Danilo Hondo was the 2002 German National Road Race Champion.

Massimo Graziato has been a stagiaire with Lampre-ISD since August of 2011 and has now being given a contract for 2012 on the basis of his performance

Davide Vigano joins Lampre-ISD for the 2012 season from Leopard Trek. He will be part of Petacchi's lead out train along with Danilo Hondo and Grega Bole.

THE TEAM: DID YOU KNOW?

Italian team, Lampre-ISD has selected the fastest riders on their roster to take to the start line of the 2012 Santos Tour Down Under with the aim of a sprint start to the season.

Lampre produces metal panels for white goods such as washing machines.
