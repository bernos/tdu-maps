VACANSOLEIL - DCM

THE RIDERS: DID YOU KNOW?

Romain Felliu, Thomas de Gendt and Sergey Lagutin all rode the 2011 Santos Tour Down Under for Vacansoleil-DCM.In 2007 Romain Feillu won the Tour of Britain. In 2008 he went on to win Stage 4 of the Tour de France claiming the yellow jersey and finishing first in the Yyoung Rider Classification for this stage.

Feillu had a strong 2011 season with three stage wins at the Tour Mediterranean (Stages 2, 3 and 4), first on Stage 5 at the Tour of Luxembourg and second place on Stage 3 of the Tour de France, just to name a few victories.

In 2011 Thomas de Gendt took stage wins at both the Tour of Switzerland and Paris - Nice.

Sergey Lagutin is the 2011 National Road Race Champion for Uzbekistan.

Sprinter, Kenny van Hummel will join Vacansoleil-DCM for the 2012 season, having signed a two year agreement.

In 2011, van Hummel was first overall at the Ronde van Drenthe and won Stage 8 at the Tour of Turkey.

Marcello Pavarin finished fifth in the Hel van het Mergelland.

Mirko Selvaggio joined Vcansoleil-DCM in 2011.

Woulter Mol won the 2010 Tour of Qatar.

THE TEAM: DID YOU KNOW?

Vacansoleil-DCM joined the ProTeam ranks in 2011, having been granted a UCI WorldTour Professional licence.

The team was founded in 2008.

The team's title sponsor is a European company which organizes luxury camping holidays.
