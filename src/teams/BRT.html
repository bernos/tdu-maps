BMC RACING TEAM

THE RIDERS: DID YOU KNOW?

Alessandro Ballan won the 2008 UCI World Road Championships, the Tour of Poland in 2009 and in 2010 finished second on Stage 15 of the Tour de France.

In 2011 Ballan finished fourth overall in the Milan- San Remo.

Adam Blythe will make his debut with the BMC Racing Team at the 2012 Santos Tour Down Under.

Marcus Burghardt won the GC sprint classification at the 2011 Tour of Belgium and finished ninth overall in the Tour of Qatar.

Martin Kohler is the current Swiss National Time Trails Champion for 2011.

Manuel Quinziato specialises in one day races and stage races. He finished second on Stage 2 of the Team Time Trials in the 2011 Tour de France.

Greg Van Avermaet will be making his first appearance at the race in 2012.

Van Avermaet had a strong 2011 with a stage win at the Tour of Austria in addition to his overall win at the Paris-Tours and Tour de Wallonie.

THE TEAM: DID YOU KNOW?

BMC Racing Team rode their first Santos Tour Down Under in 2010 as a wild card entry by invitation only.

Australia's Cadel Evans is the lead rider for BMC Racing Team and rode the 2010 Santos Tour Down Under, finishing sixth overall, and then making history by going on to be the first Australian to ever win the Tour de France in 2011.

BMC racing Team was founded in 2007 (based in the USA) competing as a UCI Professional Continental team and was first awarded a ProTeam licence in 2011.BMC Racing is a Swiss based bicycle manufacturer.
