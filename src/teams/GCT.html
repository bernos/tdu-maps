GREENEDGE CYCLING TEAM

THE RIDERS: DID YOU KNOW?

Cameron Meyer, reigning Santos Tour Down Under winner and Australian National Time Trial Champion will headline the GreenEDGE seven-rider squad for 2012 Santos Tour Down Under.

Matt Goss, who finished second at the 2011 Santos Tour Down Under, joins Meyer as an overall contender.

The GreenEDGE team line up includes three past Santos Tour Down Under winners (Cameron Meyer, Simon Gerrans and Stuart O'Grady).

Simon Gerrans, winner of the Tour Down Under in 2006, and Stuart O'Grady, twice an overall Tour Down Under winner in the race's early editions and second overall in 2009, will bolster general classification ambitions.

Robbie McEwen holds the impressive record of 12 Santos Tour Down Under stage wins, making him the only rider in the history of the event to claim this honour.

McEwen has won the Jayco Sprint General Classification (overall) at the 2002, 2004, and 2005 Santos Tour Down Under events.

Jack Bobridge is the 2011 Australian National Road Champion and the second local SA addition to the team.

Leigh Howard will make his Santos Tour Down Under debut in 2012.

Simon Gerrans is the only Australian rider to have won a stage at all three Grand Tours: Tour de France, Giro d'Italia and Vuelta a Espana.

Stuart O'Grady won the 2007 Paris Roubaix and is a three time Tour de France stage winner.

Robbie McEwen is a triple winner of the Tour de France's Green Jersey classification as well as being a multiple Tour de France and Giro d'Italia stage winner.

THE TEAM: DID YOU KNOW?

GreenEDGE Cycling is the first ever Australian professional cycling team.

In December 2011, GreenEDGE Cycling was awarded a UCI licence for the WorldTour for the 2012 and 2013 seasons.

GreenEDGE will make their WorldTour debut at the Santos Tour Down Under in 2012.

GreenEDGE launched their campaign to secure a WorldTour licence at the 2011 Santos Tour Down Under.
