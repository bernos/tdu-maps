EQUIPE CYCLISTE FDJ - BIG MAT

THE RIDERS: DID YOU KNOW?

Sandy Casar competed in the 2000 Tour Down Under, finishing fifth overall and was awarded the Cycle Instead Young Rider jersey the same year.

Casar is a Tour de France stage winner, taking stage wins on Stage 18 (2007 Tour de France), Stage 16 (2009 Tour de France) and Stage 9 (2010 Tour de France).

Veteran Frenchman, Frederic Guesdon who recently turned 40, brings a wealth of experience. Guesdon won the Paris Roubaix in 1997 and Paris Tours in 2006.

Guesdon signed with FDJ in 1997 and in 2012 will be entering his 15th season with the team.

Yahueni Hutarovich is a Vuelta a Espana stage winner (2010) and a two time National Road Champion for Belarus (2008, 2009).

Jussi Veikkanen is a five time National Road Race Champion for Finland and will be one to watch on the SKODA King of the Mountain climbs along with Jeremy Roy.

Jeremy Roy wore the King of the Mountain jersey on Stage 13 of the Tour de France in 2011.

In 2010, William Bonnet came first on Stage 2 of the Paris-Nice race.

THE TEAM: DID YOU KNOW?

Equipe Cycliste FDJ – Big Mat returns to the UCI WorldTour for 2012, after having a Professional Continental ranking in 2011.
