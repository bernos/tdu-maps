MOVISTAR TEAM

THE RIDERS: DID YOU KNOW?

Alejandro Valverde is a Grand Tour winner, taking honours at the 2009 Vuelta a Espana.

He is a three time Tour de France stage winner, has overall honours in the Liège-Bastogne-Liège in 2006 and earnt the title of UCI ProTour rider of the year in 2006 and 2008.

Jose Rojas is the current Spanish National Road Race Champion (2011).

Jose Rojas has won the Cycle Instead Young Rider classification at the 2008 and 2009 Santos Tour Down Under events.

Jose Rojas finished third overall at the 2009 Santos Tour Down Under. He finished 14th overall at the 2011 Santos Tour Down Under.

Jose Ivan Gutierrez is the 2010 Spanish National Road Race Champion - this is the second time has has taken national road race honours, winning the championship previously in 2001. He is also three time Spanish National Time trial Champion.

David Lopez won Stage 9 of the 2010 Vuelta a Espana.

Angel Madrazo won Stage 5 of the 2008 Circuit Montanes.

Angel Madrazo finished tenth overall in the Cycle Instead Young Rider Classification.

Imanol Erviti finished first in the Vuelta a la Rioja this year, giving him a taste of victory.

Javier Moreno will join Movistar for the 2012 season.

THE TEAM: DID YOU KNOW?

During the 2011 season the Movistar team raced 274,509 km and had 21 victories.

Team Movistar revealed its new look for the 2011 season in December 2010 rolling out a simple, uncluttered blue and green design with the 'M' logo of its title sponsor.

Printed on the back of the neck of the jersey is the team's motto 'Compartida, la vida es más' (Shared, life is more).
