AG2R LA MONDIALE

THE RIDERS: DID YOU KNOW?

Martin Elmiger was the overall 2007 Santos Tour Down Under winner. In 2009, he finished fourth overall in the race.

Blel Kadri finished eighth overall in the 2011 Santos Tour Down Under.

Martin Elmiger is the Swiss National Road Race Champion (2001, 2005 and 2010).

Manuel Belletti is a Giro d'Italia stage winner. He will join Ag2r La Mondiale for the 2012 season.

Kristof GODDAERT joined Ag2r in 2010.

In 2011 Blel Kadri finished second overall in the Circuit de la Sarthe.

Romain Lemarchand is the 2009 U23 French Time Trial Champion.

Matteo Montaguti was the overall winner of the 2010 Giro di Provincia di Reggio Calabria.

Boris Shpilevskiy will make his Ag2r La Mondiale 2012 season debut at the Santos Tour Down Under.

THE TEAM: DID YOU KNOW?

Ag2r La Mondiale will be the only team to have competed in all fourteen editions of the race.

The French Ag2r team has produced the most overall winners of the Santos Tour Down Under, taking out honours in 2000 (Gilles Maignan FRA), 2003 (Mikel Astarloza ESP), 2006 (Simon Gerrans AUS) and 2007 (Martin Elmiger SUI).

Ag2r won the Brilliant Blend Team Classification to make them the overall winning team at the 2010 Santos Tour Down Under.
