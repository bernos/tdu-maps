UNISA AUSTRALIA

THE RIDERS: DID YOU KNOW?

The 2012 Santos Tour Down Under will be Steee Van Hoff's first WorldTour race.

Van Hoff had a strong 2011 season with stage wins at Tour of River Murray, Tour of Geelong, Tour of Gippsland, Tour of Toowoomba, Tour of Canberra, Tour of Mersey Valley and Jayco Bay Series.

Jay McCarthy riders for Team Jayco-AIS. Team Jayco AIS is registered as a UCI Continental Team in the Oceania Region.

Rohan Dennis returns to the UniSA-Australia team in 2012, after riding with them at the 2010 Santos Tour Down Under.

Lachlan Norris finished third on both stage 3 and stage 4 of the 2011 Tour of Wellington.

In 2007, Tom Palmer became the Junior World Time Trial Champion.

The final two riders for the UniSA - Australia team will be selected after the 2012 Mars Cycling Australia National Road Championships (5 - 10 January 2012 in Ballarat, Victoria).

THE TEAM: DID YOU KNOW?

2012 will see UniSA's involvement in the Santos Tour Down Under reach a 12 year milestone.

Highlights across the past 12 years include:

Gene Bates winning the Share the Road Young Rider jersey in 2001;

The incredible 2004 win of the race overall by the retiring Patrick Jonker;

Winning the teams classification in 2006;

The most courageous ride in 42 degree heat by Paul Crake and Russel Van Hout to take second and first at Willunga in 2006;

Karl Menzies' yellow jersey assault in 2007;

The explosive riding of Jack Bobridge in 2009.

Luke Roberts taking Skoda King of the Mountain GC honours in 2011.

The UniSA Australia team is granted special permission by the UCI to compete at this WorldTour event.
