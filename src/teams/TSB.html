TEAM SAXO BANK

THE RIDERS: DID YOU KNOW?

Sergio Paulinho last rode the Santos Tour Down Under in 2005.

Paulinho is a two time Tour de France stage winner, Vuelta a Espana stage winner and two time Portugese National Champion (2004 and 2008).

Both Roberts and Cantwell join the Saxo Bank team at the 2012 Santos Tour Down Under after riding for the UniSA-Australia national team at the 2011 race.

Cantwell make his debut with Saxo Bank at the 2012 Santos Tour Down Under. With 37 victories in his career, most in Australia and the US, it will be interesting to see the impression he can make on the WorldTour stage.

In 2011, Luke Roberts won the overall Skoda King of the Mountain classification at the Santos Tour Down Under.

Anders Lund has been a professional cyclists since 2007.

In 2009 Jaroslaw Marycz took the title of Polish National Road Race Champion.

In 2001 Jonas Jorgensen won the Grand Prix d’lsbergues.

Takashi Miyazawa is the 2010 Japanese Road Race National Champion.

THE TEAM: DID YOU KNOW?

Brad McGee became a Sports Director at Team Saxo Bank in 2009. He had 17 UCI victories throughout his professional career.

In June 2008, Saxo Bank signed on as new team sponsor and are the title sponsor for the team.

New Team Saxo Bank signing for the 2012 season, Takashi Miyazawa will be sure to become a crowd favourite as the first Japanese rider to take to the start line in the history of the Santos Tour Down Under.
